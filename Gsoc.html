Mozilla Firefox presents GSOC with Firefoxes

It will be a three day long event where you will get all the insights related to Google summer of code. Our mentors will help you crack it and teach you the essentials to hack it.

About GSOC



Contrary to popular belief, Google Summer of Code is a global program focused on bringing more student developers into open source software development. Students work on a three monthprogramming project with an open source organization during their break from university. As many students takepart in this program, most have no idea how to get going.So, to get them started, we'll have a guest speaker whowould guide them with all the steps and will provide aroadmap to the program that will help students to dive in to the event head-first.


Timeline





Day-1(5/2/22)
Github Basics

Our mentor will help you to gain the basics of github. He will demonstrate all the basic commands of github which are essential for creating a repository , How to make a pull request and much more so please join us.

Day-2(6/2/22)
Intro to GSOC

Gsoc will be explained in detail by our mentor. He will explain all the intricacies involved in appearing for Gsoc and the deadlines.

Day-3(7/2/22)
Gsoc in depth

Roadmaps, tricks and tips to crack GSOC will be given by our mentor. There will also be a Question and answer session to answer all your queries.

Perks of the Event

The student will get to learn about GSOC from a person who had earlier cracked It himself with the help of his experience they can get a slight edge over other candidates.


Skill enhancement

The student's software development skills will be enhanced during this period.


Open source

Get to know more about open source through the means of our mentors.
